Detailed description:
Sets the GUI camera parameters for the scene 3D representation, with this format:
AZIM=12.34,ELEV=45.0,DIST=20.0,X=0,Y=0,Z=0,ANIM=1.0
The camera model is that of pointing to a 3D point with a given azimuth, elevation and distance to that point,with these fields (all of them are optional, and separated by commas):
  • X, Y and Z: Coordinates of the "looking-at" point.
  • AZIM: Azimuth in degrees.
  • ELEV: Elevation in degrees.
  • DIST: Distance of the camera to the "looking-at" points (the "zoom").
  • ANIM: In seconds, the time in seconds the transition will take. Default = 0.
